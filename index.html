About

This is an interactive checklist for the Billionaires Boulevard biological scanning route in Elite Dangerous, originally published on CMDR's Toolbox (cmdrs-toolbox.com), which has since been sunset.

This page was created to preserve and openly share the route data with the Elite Dangerous community so it doesn't get lost.

I am not the original author of this data. Full credit goes to the developers and contributors behind CMDR's Toolbox for compiling and publishing this route.
